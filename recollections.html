BAYSM 2013

BAYSM was founded in 2013 by Ettore Lanzarone and Francesca Ieva in Milan, Italy, which is also where the first meeting of its kind (BAYSM 2013) took place. Only one year later, BAYSM 2014 followed in Vienna, Austria, and Raffaele Argiento, Angela Bitto, Gregor Kastner, Kristina Lurz, and Alexandra Posekany joined the BAYSM board. The next meeting, BAYSM 2016, took place in Florence, Italy, followed by BAYSM 2018 in Warwick, UK, where Daniele Durante and Sara Wade joined as organizers. During that time, BAYSM became the official conference of j-ISBA.
